## 📋 **All Rules Implemented:**

### **💰 Payment & Rent Rules:**
1. **Monthly Rent Payment** - Due before 5th, ₹100/day late fee
2. **Deposit Refund** - ₹1,000 deduction on leaving
3. **No Refund Policy** - No refunds once paid
4. **Extended Stay** - ₹300/day for extensions
5. **Partial Month** - ₹300/day for partial months

### **📋 Notice & Vacating:**
1. **15-day advance notice** required (online only)
2. **Website registration** mandatory for notices

### **🔒 Security & Safety:**
1. **Valuables security** - Keep in lockers, PG not responsible
2. **Personal belongings** disclaimer
3. **Keys responsibility** - Replacement cost on resident
4. **Prohibited items** - No weapons, drugs, etc.

### **👥 Conduct & Behavior:**
1. **Violence policy** - Management action rights
2. **Opposite gender** - Not allowed, immediate termination
3. **General conduct** - No offensive behavior
4. **Police encounters** - Must inform management

### **👥 Visitors & Guests:**
1. **Permission required** - ID and address proof needed
2. **Guest charges** - ₹400/day
3. **Visitor liability** - Resident responsible for guest actions

### **🏠 Facilities & Maintenance:**
1. **Electrical appliances** - No heating devices in rooms
2. **Washing machine** - No overloading, immediate unloading
3. **Water conservation** - Report leakages immediately
4. **Maintenance access** - PG can enter for repairs
5. **Housekeeping** - May clean in absence, secure valuables

### **🚨 Emergency & Crisis:**
1. **Emergency procedures** - Follow management guidelines
2. **Feedback system** - Use website complaints section
